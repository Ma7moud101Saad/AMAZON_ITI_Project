Multi-platform compatibility, ArkarTech N11 headphone will fit well with most of your devices:
How to connect it to PS4:
1. Insert the headset’s 3.5mm jack into gamepad;
2. Enter the System Settings by long pressing the PS4 button;
3. Choose "PERIPHERAL MACHINES," then "ADJUST SOUND AND DEVICES," then "Output to the headphones," then change it to "All audio."
How to connect to Xbox One:
Check your Xbox One controller first. If it has a 3.5mm audio interface, you can use this headset directly. If it doesn't have a 3.5mm interface, and only has an expansion port, you will need a Microsoft adapter (NOT included) and then connect the headset to the adapter.
How to connect to PC:
Please use the 1-to-2 3.5mm jack splitter cable if your desktop has separate audio and microphone interfaces.
